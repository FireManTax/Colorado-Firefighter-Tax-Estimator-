Overtime Pay & the “Big Beautiful Bill”

How overtime income is taxed

Overtime pay is fully taxable under federal and Colorado law. No law has made overtime income tax-free.

Overtime often appears overtaxed because payroll systems annualize high-earning pay periods, which increases withholding.

When you file your tax return, your actual annual income determines your true tax rate.
